FIRE

Fire signs of the zodiac are: Aries, Leo & Sagittarius. They are generally active, passionate, competitive, impatient and quick-tempered. These signs embody masculine (yang) energy.

EARTH

Earth signs of the zodiac are: Taurus, Virgo & Capricorn. They are generally pragmatic, dependable, realistic, inflexible, and ruthlessly stubborn. They embody feminine (yin) energy.

AIR

Air signs of the zodiac are: Gemini, Libra & Aquarius. They are generally charming, logical, versatile, indecisive, and fickle. These signs embody masculine (yang) energy.

WATER

Water signs of the zodiac are: Cancer, Scorpio & Pisces. They are generally emotional, intuitive, mysterious, moody and hypersensitive. They embody feminine (yin) energy.
